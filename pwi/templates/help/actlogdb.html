P-WI Actual/Logical DB Editor Interface functionality

General Description

The Actual/Logical DB module is a search, display and edit page all in one. A user must be logged in to edit but can search without logging in.

  • Thistle = search only
  • Green = search & edit
  • Yellow = current row

Supported Browsers

  • Chrome and FireFox are supported on both PC and Mac computers.
  • Safari is NOT supported for the PWI. There are features that do not work.

Searching

All fields that have labels highlighted in green may be used for searching. They may be searched individually or in combination. To include data in a tab in a search: click on the tab to expand it and enter text to be searched. When more than one field is used in a search, it is treated as an AND search.

Wildcards

  • Wildcards (%) may be used for most fields.
  • A wildcard may be used at the beginning, middle and/or end of your search text: e.g. %paired%, casein% or AK32%43%.

Search by Dates

  • Valid date formats for searching are:
    • mm/dd/yyyy or mm-dd-yyyy
    • mm/dd/yy or mm-dd-yy
    • yyyy/mm/dd or yyyy-mm-dd
  • Searching for a range of dates:
    • equal to: enter a single date
    • greater than: > mm/dd/yyyy
    • greater than or equal to: >= mm/dd/yyyy
    • less than: < mm/dd/yyyy
    • less than or equal to: <= mm/dd/yyyy
    • between two dates (inclusive): mm/dd/yyyy..mm/dd/yyyy
