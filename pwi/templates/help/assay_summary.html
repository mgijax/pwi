P-WI Expression Assay Summary functionality:

Includes all types of assays, both GXD and Cre.

Sort order: (1)Gene, (2)Assay type, (3)Citation, by author, (4)Assay ID.

Only the first 5,000 matching records are displayed. To get all results if more than 5,000 records match, click the "View All Assays" link at the top of the table, or download the tab-delimited file.
