P-WI Probe Detail Functionality:

  • Displays molecular probe/clone data.
  • Includes all fields from the EI unless stated below.
  • Names are displayed verbatim.
  • Primers include fewer fields than cDNA, genomic, and other probe types.
  • Raw Sequence Notes are not displayed.
  • In general, fields are labeled and presented in the same order as in the corresponding EI Modules.
Associated Data
  • Other Accession IDs - from other providers
  • Markers - associated with the probe record
  • References
    • References are listed chronologically from oldest to newest.
    • Data associated with a Reference are shown:
      • Sequence IDs - linked to Public MGI Sequence Detail page
      • Endonuclease (RFLP) data - Endonuclease, Marker, Allele, Fragments, and Strains (with MGI markup translated to superscript)
      • Aliases
      • Notes - the general Probe Notes field that is also displayed on Public
  • Other Summary Links
    • Expression Assays - assay records with which the probe is associated
