P-WI Expression Results Summary functionality:

  • Includes all types of assay results, both GXD and Cre.
  • Sort order:
    • Results for a reference: (1)TS/structure, (2) Gene, (3) Assay type, (4) MGI ID of assay, (5) Specimen label
    • Results for markers and structures: (1) Assay type, (2) stage, (3) structure, (4) Expressed? yes/no
  • Assay types are ordered by _Assay_key value; Cre assay results appear at the end of the table:
    • _AssayType_key | assayType
    • 6 | Immunohistochemistry
    • 1 | RNA in situ
    • 9 | In situ reporter (knock in)
    • 2 | Northern blot
    • 8 | Western blot
    • 5 | RT-PCR
    • 4 | RNase protection
    • 3 | Nuclease S1
    • 10 | In situ reporter (transgenic)
    • 11 | Recombinase reporter
    • -2 | Not Applicable
    • -1 | Not Specified
  • Assay results are paginated - 250 results per page. Results may also be downloaded as a tab-delimited file.
  • Supported URLs include:
    • Assay results for a Marker: /pwi/summary/result?marker_id=MGI:(number)
    • Assay results for a Reference: /pwi/summary/result?refs_id=J:(number)
